    Did you know?

    Washington would occasionally end the meal with a toast. He would lift his glass and say, "To all our friends." The guests then would respond by saying "Huzzah!" three times and then everyone at the table took a sip of their drink.

    "Huzzah, huzzah, huzzah!"
