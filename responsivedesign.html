About Me

I am a M.A. student in History at GMU. My primary interest is in the social and cultural history of the 18th and early 19th century South.

This Page

This page will chronicle my work for the course HIST 697: Creating History in New Media at George Mason University in Fairfax, VA. The aim of the course is to have students produce a professional digital history website by the end of the semester.
