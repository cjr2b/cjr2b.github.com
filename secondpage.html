About Me

I am a M.A. student in History at George Mason University. My primary interest is in the eighteenth and early nineteenth-century South. I have worked at numerous historical sites throughout the Washington, D.C. metro area in order to pursue my passion for historical research, interpretation and material culture. The nitty-gritty of it is….I enjoy "old stuff" and I want others to be able enjoy, learn from, and appreciate our past through these objects too.

This means you may see me in colonial-period clothing on any given weekend

So here is my quest to earn some HTML and CSS savvy for the pursuit of history.
